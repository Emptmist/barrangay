Sa darating na June 28, 2023 (Wednesday) ang Sangguniang Kabataan katuwang ang Sangguniang Barangay at Barangay Anti-Drug Abuse Council (BADAC) ng Barangay Molino 4 ay magsasagawa ng 𝘼𝙉𝙏𝙄-𝙄𝙇𝙇𝙀𝙂𝘼𝙇 𝘿𝙍𝙐𝙂 𝘼𝘽𝙐𝙎𝙀 𝘼𝙒𝘼𝙍𝙀𝙉𝙀𝙎𝙎 𝙋𝙍𝙊𝙂𝙍𝘼𝙈 𝙏𝙊 𝙈𝙊𝙇𝙄𝙉𝙊 𝙄𝙑 𝙔𝙊𝙐𝙏𝙃 𝘼𝙉𝘿 𝙍𝙀𝙎𝙄𝘿𝙀𝙉𝙏𝙎, in observance of the International Day Against Drug Abuse and Illicit Trafficking (IDADAIT) 2023.
This year’s World Drug Day is a call to:
1. Raise awareness about the negative impact of stigma and discrimination on people who use drugs and their families.
Raise awareness about the AIDS and hepatitis epidemics among people who use drugs and expand and strengthen HIV and hepatitis prevention programmes.
2. Promote evidence-based, voluntary services for all people who use drugs.
3. Educate about drug use disorders, available treatments and the importance of early intervention and support.
4. Advocate for alternatives to imprisonment for drug-related crimes, such as community-based treatment and services.
5. Combat stigma and discrimination by promoting language and attitudes that are respectful and non-judgmental.
6. Empower young people and communities to prevent drug use and addiction.
Sama-sama po nating suportahan ang adhikain ng UNODC - United Nations Office on Drugs and Crime at Dangerous Drugs Board, kaya naman iniimbitahan po namin ang ating mga kabataan at ka-barangay na dumalo at maki-isa sa ating programa na gaganapin sa ating Barangay Molino IV Covered Court, 2:00 ng hapon.
